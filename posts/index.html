Simplifying hugo writing on your phone

EasyHugo Simplifying Hugo Writing on Your Phone! EasyHugo is a streamlined mobile app designed specifically for Hugo users, offering a clean and intuitive interface to help you write and manage Hugo posts anytime, anywhere. With Markdown support, you can easily capture life’s moments on the go, and even enhance your posts by inserting images effortlessly. Key Features Multi-Site Management: Seamlessly manage multiple Hugo websites in one app. Markdown Editing: Enjoy smooth, desktop-like Markdown editing right on your phone. Post Preview: Instantly preview your posts as you write, with a true “what you see is what you get” experience. Image Insertion: Easily add images to bring a personal touch to your posts. Git Syncing: Sync your content with your Git repository in one tap, giving you full control over your data and ensuring privacy. Multi-Language Support: Available in 12 languages for a global user experience. Dark Mode: Switch to a dark theme for a comfortable visual experience anytime. EasyHugo is a powerful and focused tool for managing Hugo content on mobile, combining the freedom of mobile writing with secure data control.
